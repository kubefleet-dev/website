{{% blocks/custom-showcase color="primary" %}}
KubeFleet provides at-scale <b>multi-cluster Kubernetes resources management</b>. Use KubeFleet to safely and intelligently schedule resources across any member cluster, <b>maximizing your infrastructure investment</b> while reducing your management overheads. KubeFleet works with any CNCF-certified Kubernetes release <b>on any cloud, on-premises or on the edge</b>.
{{< /blocks/custom-showcase >}}

{{% blocks/section color="dark" type="row" %}}
{{% blocks/feature icon="fab fa-hubspot" title="Centralized management" %}}
KubeFleet acts as a multi-cluster control plane, enabling anyone to easily deploy and manage any resource on any cluster.
{{% /blocks/feature %}}

{{% blocks/feature icon="fa-solid fa-brain" title="Smart scheduling"%}}
Leverage KubeFleet's flexible scheduling capabilities to always run workloads in the most appropriate clusters, maximizing performance, availiability and cost efficiency.
{{% /blocks/feature %}}

{{% blocks/feature icon="fa-solid fa-bars-progress" title="Simplified administration" %}}
Use KubeFleet to perform administrative tasks with ease. KubeFleet can help you enforce policies, observe status, detect configuration drift, and move resources around as needed.
{{% /blocks/feature %}}
